{flavor:Project Awakening ECT procedure for disciplinary action, annotated by Doctor Herman Carter himself. The intolerably high voltage leaves the patient in a severe state of paranoia.}

Considerably increases Madness inflicted by Shock Therapy.

Survivors with Madness suffer from the following Afflictions:
Madness II: When a Chase begins, Survivors perceive the Red Stain and Terror Radius as though The Doctor were right behind them for a duration of 10 seconds.
Madness III: When a Chase begins, Survivors constantly perceive the Red Stain and Terror Radius as though The Doctor were right behind them.
The Doctor shares in his patients' Madness in order to see the Illusionary Red Stain.

Afflictions of the same type do not stack.
