{flavor:An anti-haemorrhagic substance that stops the bleeding in a matter of seconds.}

Use with Secondary Action.
The affected Survivor will passively gain one Health State 16 seconds after use.
The time required can be modified by Perks and Add-ons that affect Healing speeds.
This effect is cancelled when the affected Survivor changes their Health State or is picked up by the Killer.

{this} depletes the Med-Kit on use.
