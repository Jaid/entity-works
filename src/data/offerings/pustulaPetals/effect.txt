Calls upon The Entity to generate 1 Cankerous Hook and 1 Cankerous Generator.

As a Killer, hook a Survivor on a Cankerous Hook for the first time to earn bonus Bloodpoints.
As a Survivor, find and repair a Cankerous Generator to earn bonus Bloodpoints.
Stacks.
Using this Offering increases the amount of Bloodpoints earned for these interactions.

Note: During the Event, 1 default Cankerous Hook and 1 default Cankerous Generator will spawn on the Map.

Only playable during the The Withering Blight Event.
