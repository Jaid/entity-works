{flavor:A white powder with coagulant properties. Apply the agent to a wound to stop it from haemorrhaging.}

Use with Secondary Action.
When the {this} is used on an injured Survivor, they gain the Endurance Status Effect for 8 seconds.
Any damage taken during that time that would put the Survivor into the Dying State will instead apply the Deep Wound Status Effect.
The Survivors has then 20 seconds to Mend themselves.
If the Survivor takes any damage while affected by Deep Wound or if the Deep Wound timer ends, they will immediately be put into the Dying State.

{this} depletes the Med-Kit on use.
